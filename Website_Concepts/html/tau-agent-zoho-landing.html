FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from Zoho's built-in workflows? +

Zoho's workflows are rule-based — you build explicit if/then triggers. Tau Agent is an AI that understands intent, makes contextual decisions, and adapts. You describe what you want in plain English, and the agent handles the implementation and ongoing execution.

Does Tau Agent replace Deluge scripting? +

For most common CRM operations, yes. Lead routing, deal stage management, follow-up scheduling, and reporting no longer need custom Deluge scripts. For highly custom logic, Tau Agent works alongside your existing scripts.

What Zoho CRM editions does it support? +

Tau Agent works with Zoho CRM Professional, Enterprise, and Ultimate editions. Standard edition has limited API access, so some features may not be available.

Can I customize the AI's decisions? +

Yes. You define scoring rules, assignment logic, escalation thresholds, and reporting criteria. The agent operates within your guardrails — it's autonomous, not unsupervised.

Is my CRM data safe? +

We use OAuth-only access to your Zoho account. We never store your CRM data — the agent reads and writes directly to Zoho's API. All actions are logged and reversible.

How long does setup take? +

Most teams are running their first automation within 15 minutes. Connect your Zoho org, describe what you want automated, and the agent starts working. Enterprise setups with complex custom modules typically take 1–2 weeks.

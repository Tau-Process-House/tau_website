FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from Zapier or Make? +

Zapier and Make are rule-based — you build explicit if/then flows. Tau Agent is an AI agent that understands intent, makes decisions, and adapts in real time. You describe what you want, and the agent figures out how to do it.

Can I customize the AI's decision-making? +

Yes. You define guardrails, approval thresholds, escalation rules, and decision boundaries. The agent operates within your policies — it's autonomous, not unsupervised.

What happens if the agent makes a mistake? +

Every action is logged in the audit trail and reversible. You can set confidence thresholds where the agent pauses and asks a human to confirm before proceeding.

How long does setup take? +

Most teams are running their first workflow within 30 minutes. Enterprise deployments with SSO and custom connectors typically take 1–2 weeks with our onboarding team.

Is my data used to train AI models? +

No. Your data is yours. We never use customer data for model training. All data is encrypted, isolated, and stored in your chosen region (EU or US).

What support do you offer? +

Starter includes email support. Growth gets priority response within 4 hours. Enterprise includes a dedicated account manager and custom SLA with phone support.
